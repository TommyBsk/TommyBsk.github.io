I'm a PhD candidate in the [Ph.D. program of national  interest in Robotics and Intelligent Machines](https://drim.i-rim.it/en/) at the [Italian Institute of Technology](https://www.iit.it/it/home). I'm advised by Prof. [Alessio Del Bue](https://www.iit.it/it/people-details/-/people/alessio-delbue) and Prof. [Pietro Morerio](https://www.iit.it/it/people-details/-/people/pietro-morerio) in the [PAVIS lab](https://pavis.iit.it/)
and by Prof. [Lorenzo Natale](https://www.iit.it/it/people-details/-/people/lorenzo-natale) in the [HSP lab](https://hsp.iit.it/home).

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, natural language understanding, and robotics. In particular, my work focuses on developing Embodied AI agents that can navigate and interact with their surroundings while building a rich semantic understanding of 3D environments. This involves enhancing their perception and reasoning capabilities to recognize objects, interpret spatial relationships, and generate meaningful linguistic descriptions, even in cluttered indoor spaces with occlusions and partial views.

Previously, I was a deep learning researcher at the [San Raffaele Research Hospital](https://research.hsr.it/en/index.html), where I worked on medical image analysis, developing AI models for automated diagnosis and interpretation of medical imaging data.
